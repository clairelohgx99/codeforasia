ABOUT

"Asia is Poor — The need to establish Asia as a continent of opportunity in the global eye"

InnovAsian is an alliance of changemakers who are co-creating a future in which youths are game and ready to co-create the future of Asia. Borne in the digital age and conceived by a network of educators, entrepreneurs and technologists, we are inviting all who are interested to join us on this journey to shape a shared future together. Many of the biggest challenges that we face as a generation are problems of collective action and we cannot solve our problems with the same thinking we used when we created them. InnovAsian is thus a call-to-action for youths, young working professionals and interested organisations alike to change the system of youths’ overseas engagement together. We are bringing to fruition a future in which youths are effective cross-cultural leaders leveraging the best that technology has to offer to create and scale positive impacts for the world, where young working professionals are connected to youths to offer actual advice and support from the frontline demands of work and we want you to get involved. Come explore how we can create systems change by design together!

InnovAsian is a community campus for a global age. Over the years, InnovAsian has pass through five phases which consist of seed, supply, sustainable, sacle, and evolving. InnovAsia make product for portfolio execution, demand generation via stories, and build though leadersip in the first phase. Afterwards, InnovAsia started to do enhancement sucha as professional upgrading and onversion. Not just that, it is trying strive to get employer endorsement and research collaboration during its second phase. When it goes into the third phase, InnovAsia aimed for sustainable which make its platform open for public contributions because they want to be entirely self-funded. Further, InnovAsian open source toolkits and templates in order to benefit more people in its fourth phase. In the fifth phase, InnovAsian make better evolves and let the community review of its Mission, Vision, Structure, and Thematic Pillars.
